BIG NEWS!!!

Sell The Heart Records is proud to announce that we are putting together a tribute album for FUGAZI. It goes without saying that Fugazi's body of work is highly influential and celebrated, but also noteworthy is the work outside of music that the band has done in bringing important political conversations to the table and inspiring countless numbers of people to become engaged in their communities.

What makes this project even more exciting is that it will serve as a fundraising effort to contribute to Taking It To The Streets, a San Francisco based non-profit that focuses on helping homeless youth. We are incredibly honored to be partnering with them on this, and feel that this is a great way to envoke the spirit of what Fugazi stands for on top of paying tribute to them through the album itself.

The album will be a compilation of thoughtfully done covers by a variety of artists. To keep from spoiling too much, we're initially announcing only a few of the artists, but we will be periodically announcing additional contributing acts as we get closer to the release of the album, which is scheduled to be in March of 2017. For this initial announcement, we're psyched to announce that Great Apes, SNIPERS!!, Black Map, Know Secrets, Jonah Matranga, and Vast Wild have all agreed to contribute a song to this project. We will be launching a crowdfunding campaign over the next week to help raise funds for this project so check back over the next few days for additional information. We will announce more bands over the next few weeks.
