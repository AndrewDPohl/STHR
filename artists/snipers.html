SNIPERS!! began as a side project of drummer Terrance Fleming and guitarist Andy Pohl's band, Good City Lie Still. After Good City's break up in 2008, the pair continued the project as their main creative outlet. Originally a two piece, the band has seen a rotating cast of characters over it's lifespan, ultimately landing as a four-piece which included guitarist Derek King and bassist Ben Shackelford. Let The Wolves Eat Themselves was recorded at Derek's home studio, and was Sell The Heart Records' first official release.
