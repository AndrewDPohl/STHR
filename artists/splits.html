Brian Moss (Hanalei) and Chris McCaughan (Sundowner) have been friends for a number of years, having met while both living in Chicago. Both men have cut their teeth in some awesome bands over the years: The Lawrence Arms and The Broadways for McCaughan, while Moss did time in The Wunder Years, The Ghost, and currently fronts Great Apes. This 7" split features a cover of Kate Bush's "Hounds Of Love" from Hanalei, and an original song from Sundowner titled "Inside The Bones". Sell The Heart co-released this split with Mission Social Club
