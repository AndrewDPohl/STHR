Rancid’s genre-defining 1995 album, ...And Out Come The Wolves, was an unexpected, cataclysmal explosion of punk into the mainstream. Countless bands and musicians since, from Anti-Flag to Worriers, have cited this record as their introduction to the genre and the entire world of punk music and DIY. To honor Wolves’ impact on the music we make today, nineteen bands have come together to reimagine the album, connected through the DIY ethos they learned from this record. ...And Out Come The Lawsuits! is a 25th anniversary album running on punk time, releasing June 4th, 2021* on Lavasocks and Sell The Heart Records.

The album is available to pre-order it's 2nd pressing from both Sell The Heart and Lavasocks Records in a White Hot variant. Artists contributing to the album include: Link 80, Worriers, The Hammerbombs, Get Married, Matamoska, Bad Idols, Flying Raccoon Suit, Pretty Frankenstein and more.
