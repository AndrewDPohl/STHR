Beard-os from Sonoma County!! With riffs that destroy walls, vocals that would frighten a bear in the best way possible, and musicianship of the highest caliber, OVER THE FALLS paired with Sell The Heart for their 2011 album YES, HAVE SOME. Comprised of Bret Fontaine (guitar/vocals), Ryan Alderman (drums), Chris Rodgers (vocals) and Tim Bastianon (bass/vocals), these fellas have released one other full length and an EP, which can be found HERE. For fans of the mighty Clutch, Faith No More, ZZ Top, and of course PRIMUS.
