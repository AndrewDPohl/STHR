BIG NEWS!!!

Sell The Heart Records is proud to announce that we are putting together a tribute album for FUGAZI. It goes without saying that Fugazi's body of work is highly influential and celebrated, but also noteworthy is the work outside of music that the band has done in bringing important political conversations to the table and inspiring countless numbers of people to become engaged in their communities.

What makes this project even more exciting is that it will serve as a fundraising effort to contribute to Taking It To The Streets, a San Francisco based non-profit that focuses on helping homeless youth. We are incredibly honored to be partnering with them on this, and feel that this is a great way to envoke the spirit of what Fugazi stands for on top of paying tribute to them through the album itself.

The album will be a compilation of thoughtfully done covers by a variety of artists. Some of the bands that have agreed to contribute include:

  • Great Apes covering "Runaway Return" off of Steady Diet of Nothing
  • SNIPERS!! covering "Arpeggiator" off of End Hits
  • Screw 32 covering "Public Witness Program" off of In On The Kill Taker
  • Identical Homes covering "Long Division" off of Steady Diet of Nothing
  • Black Map covering "Life and Limb" off of The Argument
  • Vast Wild covering "Waiting Room" off of 13 Songs
  • Jonah Matranga covering "Suggestion" off of 13 Songs
  • Casey Chisholm covering "Sweet and Low" off of In On The Kill Taker
  • SayThing covering "Closed Captioned" off of End Hits
  • The Down House covering "Give Me The Cure" off of 13 Songs
  • Warsaw covering "Merchandise" off of Repeater
  • Lynne Angel covering "Kyeo" off of Steady Diet of Nothing
  • The Distant Intimate covering "Returning The Screw" off of In On The Kill Taker
  • My Last Line covering "Bad Mouth" off of 13 Songs
  • Long Knives covering "Target" off of Red Medicine
  • Know Secrets covering "Cashout" off of The Argument
  • Gnarboots covering "I'm So Tired" off of the Instrument Soundtrack
  • theSTART covering "Place Position" off of End Hits
  • No Friends covering "The Argument" off of The Argument
